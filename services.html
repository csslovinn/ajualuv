Individual & Group Services

Energy Healing

Ajualuv draws on both ancient and modern methods of energy healing to balance and de-stress the body. The base of our practice is rooted in the Usui System of Natural Healing developed by Mikao Usui in the early 1920's. This practice uses light touch to bring alignment back to the spirit, soul, and body. During a session, we will discuss with the client their intention for treatment and provide personalized follow-up material to help the client further develop their goals. We offer in-person treatments from our East Falls location for $80 an hour, distance treatments for $20 (lasts 20 minutes), and home treatments for $110 an hour. Discounts are available for students, referrals, and those experiencing financial hardship.

Contact Ajualuv about energy healing & Reiki.

Art Therapy

Ajualuv uses a variety of both expressive arts (collage, painting, journaling, and music) and therapeutic arts (meditation, creative visualization, Reiki, and yoga) to create dialogue with clients focusing on self-awareness and accessing divine wisdom from within. The purpose is to assist clients in constructing personalized spirit-based tools that empower their whole being and stimulates inner potential. We are not affiliated with any religion, but believe that a healthy balance of spirit, soul, and body creates the optimal environment for growth and healing. We offer one-on-one sessions for private clients, pro bono group sessions for youth organizations, and Skype sessions for long distance clients. Discuss with us your objectives and we will tailor the session to meet your desired outcome. Private one-on-one and group sessions are $75 an hour and include all art supplies. Discounts are available for students and those experiencing financial hardship.

Contact Ajualuv about art therapy.

About Ajua Hawkins

Ajua Hawkins is a writer and artist focusing on holistic health and spirituality. She holds degrees in the African way of life, Religion, and man's Law from Tufts University, the School of Oriental and African Studies (University of London), and Howard University School of Law. A trained painter from the age of 7, Ajua continued her studies in art at programs held at Cornell University and Parsons School of Design. Ajua is a Reiki Level II practitioner trained at The Reiki School and Clinic in Philadelphia, PA and Holistic Passages in Woodbury, NJ.
