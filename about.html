Ajualuv Ascension Essentials

Ajualuv Ascension Essentials provides energy healing, art therapy, interactive workshops and lectures that focus on holistic health. We craft our services by taking into consideration the interplay of our spirits, souls, and bodies on our overall well-being. Ajualuv emphasizes the importance of self-awareness and knowledge of self as a means of accessing greater wisdom from within.

Our mission at Ajualuv is to:

  • 1) assist our clients in discerning and trusting their intuition,
  • 2) create a knowledge base for our clients to promote self-empowerment and healing, and
  • 3) encourage clients to live authentic lives to enable them to unlock untapped potential.

Thank you for visiting. Wishing you so much love and peace on your journey. Remember, your body is your temple, fill it with love.

About Ajua Hawkins

Ajua Hawkins is a writer and artist focusing on holistic health and spirituality. She holds degrees in the African way of life, Religion, and man's Law from Tufts University, the School of Oriental and African Studies (University of London), and Howard University School of Law. A trained painter from the age of 7, Ajua continued her studies in art at programs held at Cornell University and Parsons School of Design. Ajua is a Reiki Level II practitioner trained at The Reiki School and Clinic in Philadelphia, PA and Holistic Passages in Woodbury, NJ.
